# Ideas

## Purpose

Reference/inspiration images for the planned "Learn Italian with Lara"
website, collected before any code or design tooling exists in this repo.
Treat these as raw input for future design and copywriting decisions, not as
a finished spec.

## Contents

- **`hero_idea.jpeg`** — Hero banner v1: teal background, Lara's photo, and
  headline "Learn Italian with a Native Italian Teacher".
- **`hero_v2.jpeg`** — Hero/logo concept v2: minimal wordmark "Learn 🥂talian
  with Lara" with an outline of Italy and a spritz emoji. Reads as a
  logo/branding exploration rather than a full hero section.
- **`idea.jpeg`** — Full landing-page hero mockup: pain-point hook bubbles
  ("Does grammar feel overwhelming?", "Dreaming of having real conversations
  in Italian?", "Planning trip or moving to Italy?", "Do you want to
  understand Italians beyond the textbook?") leading to a "Tell me more" CTA,
  next to Lara's photo.
- **`my_method.jpeg`** — Feature grid for a "method" section: Speak from Day
  One, Interactive & Fun, Authentic Italian, Conversation-Based, Tailored to
  You, Italian for Real Life.
- **`my_method_v2.jpeg`** — Alternate/extended "method" section: bilingual
  (IT/EN) copy, a "Why they work" rationale list, and a "Risultati" (results)
  block split by beginner vs. intermediate learners.
- **`about_me.jpeg`** — "About" bio copy: "Ciao! Sono Lara" — BA in
  Linguistic and Cultural Mediation, MA in Languages and Cultures for
  Communication and International Cooperation, MA in Interpreting; lived and
  worked in the UK and Russia; teaching international students since 2022;
  DILS-PG Level II certification in teaching Italian as a foreign language
  (2025).

## Key concepts

- Files with a `_v2` suffix (`hero_v2`, `my_method_v2`) are alternates of the
  `_idea`/base version, not later authoritative revisions — no image
  indicates which variant (if either) was chosen. Read them as competing
  options.
- Copy mixes English and Italian inconsistently across images; audience
  language targeting has not been decided.
